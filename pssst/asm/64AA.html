64AA: Play square wave sound
Used by the routines at 6473 and 64B8.
Input
D Duration of wave
64AA LD A,$10 A=10 (speaker on = bit 4).
64AC OUT ($FE),A
64AE LD B,D Decrease duration by one and loop back to 64AF until counter is zero.
64AF DJNZ $64AF
64B1 XOR A Speaker off.
64B2 OUT ($FE),A
64B4 LD B,D Decrease duration by one and loop back to 64B5 until counter is zero.
64B5 DJNZ $64B5
64B7 RET Return.
