9BD2: Calculate Attribute Address
Used by the routines at MenuWriteText, HandlerFoodItem, SetKeyPositions, SetEntityAttributes, PrintStringColour and DrawSidePanelAttrs.
Input

Output

Converts a given pixel address to the associated attribute buffer address.
