Platforms

The graphics for the platforms are held at UDG_Platform, 76EA and 76F2.

They are simple 8x8 pixel sized character blocks, consisting simply of a left piece, a middle piece, and an end piece.
These are repeated patterns for both the platforms and the planets surface.
