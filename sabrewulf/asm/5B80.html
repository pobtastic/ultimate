5B80: Decryption Routine
Runs "CPL" on every byte from 6000-6000.
Decryption 5B80 NOP HL=GameEntry.
5B81 NOP
5B82 NOP
Decryption_Loop 5B83 NOP A=HL.
5B84 NOP CPL operation.
5B85 NOP Write A back to HL.
5B86 NOP Increment HL by one.
5B87 NOP Return.
